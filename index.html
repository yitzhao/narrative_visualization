Car Purchase Recommendations

This narrative visualization recommends fuel-efficient cars to users.
It consists of four scenes. The first scene, City vs Highway MPG, provides a general overview of fuel-efficient cars, with annotations highlighting the most efficient ones. The remaining three scenes allow users to explore specific car types in more detail, focusing on car makes, fuel types, and the number of cylinders, with annotations recommending efficient cars in these dimensions.
There are two types of user interactions enabled. In the first and last scenes, users can hover over the points to see detailed information about each car, such as make and fuel type. In the second and third scenes, users can hover over the bars to see exact values. Additionally, in the second to fourth scenes, users can select the type of MPG (city or highway) to further investigate car efficiencies.
