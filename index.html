Car Purchase Recommendations

This narrative visualization recommends fuel-efficient cars to the audience.
It consists of four scenes. The first scene, City vs Highway MPG, provides a general understanding of which cars are efficient. The remaining three scenes allow users to explore car types of interest in more detail, focusing on car makes, fuel types, and the number of cylinders.
In the first scene, users can hover over the points to see detailed information about each car, such as make and fuel type. In the second to fourth scenes, users can select the type of MPG (city or highway) to further investigate car efficiencies.
